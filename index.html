PMM DAO (Decentralized Autonomous Organization) is an organizational form based on blockchain technology, operating differently from traditional centralized organizations.

Decision Making

DAO decisions are typically formulated through smart contracts. Members can submit proposals, which include detailed information about organizational operations, development, investments, or other transactions.

Voting

Members vote on proposals, and each member's voting power is usually proportional to the amount of tokens they hold in the DAO. Members with more tokens have a greater voting influence, reflecting a governance model based on financial contribution.

Smart Contract Execution

Once a proposal is approved through voting, relevant smart contracts execute the associated actions. This may involve transferring funds, carrying out a specific task, or changing the organization's rules.

Our Working Process

Community Building

Attract and cultivate an actively engaged community through social media, online forums, and offline events. Community members will become participants in the PMM DAO, involved in project decision-making and promotional activities.

Proposal Submission

Community members can submit proposals for promoting the project. These proposals may include marketing campaigns, social media promotions, strategic partnerships, and more. Proposals should be clear and specific for other members to vote on.

Token Distribution

Distribute the project's tokens to PMM DAO members as an incentive for participation and contribution. This can be achieved through airdrops, bounty programs, or other reward mechanisms to ensure community members have a stake and engagement in the project.
